Robotic Gripper Arm

The task was to design a robotic gripper mechanism attached to a pendulum to hold an irregular object. Once the object was within the gripper the pendulum was let go from a maximum height for a period of two oscillations. The object was not allowed more than a 0.1in deflection from the original position.
